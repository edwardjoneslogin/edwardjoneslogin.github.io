What is Edward Jones?

Edward Jones is a private financial services firm founded in 1922 by Edward D. Jones in St. Louis, Missouri, United States. The company focuses on serving individual investors and business owners. With a vast network of over 15,000 locations and 19,000 financial advisors, Edward Jones manages approximately $1.8 trillion in assets worldwide. It provides personalized advice and solutions related to certificates of deposit, bonds, stocks, and mutual funds. Notably, Edward Jones is owned exclusively by its employees and retired employees, making it a unique player in the financial industry.

How to Access Edward Jones Login?

  1. Go to the Edward Jones website at www.edwardjones.com.
  2. Click on the 'Client Login' button in the top right corner of the page.
  3. Enter your username and password in the appropriate fields. If you have forgotten your username or password, click on the 'Forgot Username' or 'Forgot Password' link and follow the instructions to retrieve or reset them.
  4. Once you have successfully logged in, you will be taken to the Edward Jones Client Portal. From here, you can view your account information, conduct transactions, and access other account features.
  5. If you have multiple accounts with Edward Jones, you can view them all in the Client Portal by clicking on the 'Accounts' tab at the top of the page.
  6. To log out of the Edward Jones Client Portal, click on the 'Logout' button in the top right corner of the page.

How to Reset your Edward Jones Login Password?

  1. Visit the Edward Jones Account Access page.
  2. Click on “Forgot user ID or password?”
  3. Enter your User ID, Date of Birth (mm/dd/yyyy), and the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number.
  4. Create a new password and retype it.
  5. Optionally, set a password hint for future reference.
